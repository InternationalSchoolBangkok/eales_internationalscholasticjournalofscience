About the Journal

An important component of the Physics program at International School Bangkok is experimental research. All students conduct and report on several pieces of original research during the course of their studies. The ISB Journal of Physics provides a forum in which students may publish their research. The papers have been peer reviewed by the editors and have been judged correct and substantive.

The Journal was founded in June, 2007 by Mr. Jonathan Eales. Mr. Jonathan Eales and Dr. Ian Jacobs were the Editors of the Journal of Physics from 2007 until 2012. The Journal of Physics was published semi-annually in January and June. In January of 2013, the Journal was expanded to accept papers from all the sciences and was renamed the ISB Journal of Science. Articles in the Journal are copyrighted under Creative Commons licensing. The Journal is listed in the Directory of Open Access Journals. The Journal is archived with the National Library of Thailand, according to Thai regulations.
