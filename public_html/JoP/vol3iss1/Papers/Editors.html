Dear Reader,

Welcome to the January, 2009 issue of the International School Bangkok Journal of Physics.  The research conducted in the Physics Department at ISB last semester was varied and interesting. Research was conducted on roller coasters, complex pendulums, cavitation, resonance, bubble rings, and smoke rings. Two researchers were invited to submit their work to the Journal. The papers were peer reviewed and, after some modification, were accepted for publication in this issue.

The first paper presents research on the characteristics of standing waves in a recorder. The second paper investigates the effect of the size of turbulators on the drag coefficient of a sphere. The research was conducted by the authors and the papers were written and edited by the authors in consultation with the Editors. If you have any comments on the papers published in this issue, or would like to consult with the authors on their results, please do not hesitate to contact us.
