Editorial Policy of the Journal

Papers published in the ISB Journal of Science are the original work of students studying Science at ISB. The Editor of the Journal selects papers of interest for publication. Each paper is blind-reviewed by a member of the Peer Review Board. The Editor and Peer Reviewer consult with the authors and suggest appropriate changes, as necessary. Conclusions and views expressed remain the responsibility of the authors.

Papers are published under the Open Access Creative Commons license CC-BY, . Authors retain copyright and publishing rights to papers published in the Journal.

Further details on selection criteria and submission guidelines can be found here.

Dr. Jonathan Eales
Editor, ISB Journal of Science
