Editorial Policy of the Journal

Papers published in the ISB Journal of Physics are the original work of students studying Physics at ISB. The Editors of the Journal select papers of interest for publication. The Editors review each paper, submit the paper for blind peer-review, and then consult with the authors and suggest appropriate changes. Conclusions and views expressed remain the responsibility of the authors.

Papers are published under the Open Access Creative Commons license CC-BY, . Authors retain copyright and publishing rights to papers published in the Journal.

Further details on selection criteria and submission guidelines can be found here.

Mr. Jonathan Eales & Dr. Ian Jacobs
Editors, ISB Journal of Physics
