Dear Reader,

Welcome to the June, 2009 issue of the International School Bangkok Journal of Physics.  This is the end of our third year publishing the ISB Journal of Physics. Our upper level Physics students were in grade 9 when the Journal was founded, so have been exposed to the idea of conducting and publishing good quality research for their entire high school careers. There was a high level of interest this issue, with ten groups submitting their research for consideration.We believe we have begun to succeed in our goal of creating a culture of conducting and publishing good-quality scientific research among our Physics students.

The papers selected for publication this issue include investigations on coefficients of restitution, golf putts, electric arcing in pickles, and cushioning impacts.

The research was conducted by the authors and the papers were written and edited by the authors in consultation with the Editors. If you have any comments on the papers published in this issue, or would like to consult with the authors on their results, please do not hesitate to contact us.
