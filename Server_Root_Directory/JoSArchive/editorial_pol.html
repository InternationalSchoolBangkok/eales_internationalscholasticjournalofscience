Editorial Policy of the Journal
Papers published in the ISB Journal of Science are the original work of students studying Science at ISB. The Editor of the Journal selects papers of interest for publication. Each paper is reviewed by a member of the Peer Review Board. The Editor and Peer Reviewer consult with the authors and suggest appropriate changes. Conclusions and views expressed remain the responsibility of the authors.
